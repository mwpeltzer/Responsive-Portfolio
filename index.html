About Me

My name is Michael Peltzer and I am one of a shrinking number of people who can actually say that they grew up on a farm. My family grows citrus in the central valley here in California. You might say to yourself that this is a long way from the dusty fields and heat and you would be right.

I invite you to explore my portfolio and take a second to appreciate the juxtapostion of quite possibly two of the most different fields of expertise ever, and how this simple farm boy has now mastered the alien world of web developement.
